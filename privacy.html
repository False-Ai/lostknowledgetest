Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Lost Knowledge Archive is a student project built for educational purposes. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is protected.

Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username — displayed publicly on your entries and comments.
  • Email address — used only for account identification. Never displayed publicly.
  • Password — stored as a one-way bcrypt hash. We cannot read your password.

When you use the archive, we also store:

  • The entries, comments, and votes you submit.
  • A session cookie to keep you signed in.
  • An optional "remember me" cookie (30 days) if you choose it at login.

How we use your information

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session.
  • To display your username publicly alongside your contributions.
  • To allow you to manage, edit, and delete your entries.
  • To prevent duplicate votes per user per entry.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing.

Data storage & security

  • All data is stored in a MySQL database on the local XAMPP server.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12.
  • All database queries use PDO prepared statements to prevent SQL injection.
  • Session IDs are regenerated on login to prevent session fixation.
  • HTTP-only, SameSite cookies are used to mitigate XSS and CSRF attacks.

Your rights

  • Access — you can view all your submitted entries and comments in your dashboard.
  • Deletion — you can delete your entries from your dashboard at any time. Deleting an entry also removes all associated votes and comments.
  • Account removal — to delete your account entirely, contact the administrator.

Cookies

We use two types of cookies:

  • Session cookie — expires when you close your browser. Required to stay signed in.
  • Remember me cookie (lk_remember) — optional, lasts 30 days. Set only if you tick "Remember me" at login.

We do not use analytics cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party cookies of any kind.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data removal requests, contact the archive administrator at admin@lostknowledge.local.
